Graph Theory in Technology

Graph theory is used in many modern technologies. Below are some examples in simple words.

Search engines like Google use graph theory to rank pages. They see web pages as dots and links as lines.

Social media sites like Facebook and Twitter are huge graphs of people and their links. They use graph ideas to find key people and groups.

Some databases store data as graphs. This makes it easy to find relationships quickly.

GNNs are a type of deep learning that works on graph data. They help predict properties in networks like molecules or traffic.

Navigation apps use graph theory to find the best routes. This helps companies save time and fuel.

Graph theory is used to design networks and to detect unusual activity in cybersecurity.

In biology and medicine, graphs show how proteins interact and how diseases spread.

Graph theory is used in blockchain to track transactions and in recommendation systems to show related items.

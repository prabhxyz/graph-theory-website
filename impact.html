Impact of Graph Theory

Use the buttons below to rotate the cube and see how graph theory helps our society, culture, and economy.

Social Connectivity

Graph theory helps social media show how people are connected. It makes the idea of "six degrees of separation" real. This can help spread ideas fast. But it can also create groups that only talk to each other.

Knowledge Sharing

The web is a huge graph of pages. Graph algorithms like PageRank help find the best pages. This makes it easy for everyone to learn new things and share knowledge.

Economic Optimization

Companies use graph math to plan routes and save fuel. For example, delivery systems can cut millions of miles. This saves money and helps the environment.

Scientific Progress

Graphs help scientists see how ideas spread. They use citation graphs to know which research is very important. This guides funding and helps new discoveries.

Global Connectivity & Inequality

Graphs show which areas of the world are well connected and which are not. This helps plan new roads or internet networks, but also shows digital divides.

Negative Impacts & Cautions

The same tools that connect us can be misused. For example, data from social graphs can be used to spy on people. It is important to use graph analysis in fair and safe ways.

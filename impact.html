Impact on Society, Culture, and the Economy

Graph theory changes our world in many ways. Here are some simple ideas:

Social Connectivity

Social networks like Facebook and Twitter are built on graphs. They show how people are connected and share ideas.

Knowledge Sharing

The internet is like a giant graph of information. Graph theory helps us search and share knowledge easily.

Economic Benefits

Companies use graphs to save time and money. For example, delivery services plan routes with graphs to save fuel.

Scientific Progress

Graphs help scientists work together and spread new ideas faster. They also show which research is very important.

Global Connections

Trade and communication use graphs to link countries and people. But if one important part fails, it can affect many others.

Negative Sides

Sometimes graphs can be misused. For example, personal data in social networks may be used without permission, hurting privacy and trust.
